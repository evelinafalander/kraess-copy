Cordius Glass

The artist behind Cordius Glass is Ida Cordius.
As a designer, artist and craftsman Ida is deeply fascinated with how shapeable the warm glass is and wants to bring that aspect into her finished pieces.
With her glassware she wants to create unique and poetic pieces that moves and speaks to the viewer.
